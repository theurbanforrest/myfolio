Who We Are

Meet Piney

Piney is our mascot: A young career-driven ladder climber who suits up, grabs his coffee, and hits the professional life with energy.

Underneath the surface is a sweet and fun-loving pineapple. Life is, after all, too short to lose oneself to the grind.

RISE AND PINE embodies that spirit of finding the true balance of work & play.

Our Products

RISE AND PINE draws from the pulsing energy of the city life. Designed in New York City, our travels abroad bring additional influence from the nature-rich crispness of Portland & Seattle as well as the beach-based warmth of Los Angeles & Honolulu.

Our products feature fun designs and are intended to be used at your place of hustle. Whether it be sipping steamy coffee during a study session or rocking a trendy tee as part of your work attire, RISE AND PINE is a statement that you are hot on the trails of success.
