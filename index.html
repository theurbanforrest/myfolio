Meet Piney

Piney is our mascot: He puts on his pineapple suit on in the morning and slays the work grind in Midtown. After hours, he loosens up the tie and enjoys unwinding on the cool night scene.

He is the embodiment of that fun energy RISE AND PINE portrays through it's brand: Work Hard, Play Hard.

City Inspired

RISE AND PINE is based in New York City, the city that never sleeps and the mecca for hustlers. From the suit & tie personality of Midtown to the laid back vibes of the East Village, it is this addictive energy that only those who have been here can understand.

Our designs are a fun representation of the eclectic personalities that make up the Big (Pine)Apple.

Our Products

We are committed to sourcing locally in the U.S., choosing only the highest quality materials and manufacturers to best represent our brand.

As we expand our line (shirts are next), we promise to remain rooted in our core values and continuously provide a splash of fun for the city lifestyle.
