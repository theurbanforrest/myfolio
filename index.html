Meet Piney

Piney is our mascot: A young career-driven ladder climber who suits up, grabs his coffee, and hits the professional life with energy.

Underneath the surface is a sweet and fun-loving pineapple. Life is, after all, too short to lose oneself to the grind.

RISE AND PINE embodies that spirit of finding the true balance between work and play.

City Inspired

RISE AND PINE is based in New York City, the city that never sleeps and the mecca for hustlers.

It is the suit-tie-coffee part of our personality.

Our life experiences in Portland, Seattle, Los Angeles, and Honolulu have shown us a laid-back, sweeter side to the city life.

It is the pineapple of our personality.

This eclectic mix of work hard + be cool is the foundation for our brand and it's products.

Our Products

RISE AND PINE's offerings complement your fun personality. Our stickers add charm to any laptop or hydroflask. Our hot tumbler always gets a smile around the office.

We are committed to sourcing domestically. Quality > Quantity so we always work with small-mid sized manufacturers and producers.

As we expand our line (shirts are next), RISE AND PINE will continue to perpetuate who we are: A splash of fun in the #riseandgrind lifestyle.
