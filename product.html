Here are a few features I've worked on in my young product management career


Customer Service Ticketing System

User Story: As a Customer Rep, I want to ensure tasks requiring developer work don't get lost, So I can resolve issues in a timely fashion

Solution: Built a JIRA project to use as a ticketing system

Why I chose it: Ticketing systems ensure that any open tasks remain visible. Because JIRA is already part of our developers' workflow, it is easy to move actionable tickets into the right queues to get addressed.

How we're doing: In two months, we've closed 120 tickets and significantly improved our communication. We were also able to diagnose a handful of recurring problems and implement dev fixes for them.

Advertise Future Litters

User Story: : As a Breeder, I want to advertise puppy litters I'm expecting in the near future, So that I can build up a waiting list of qualified buyers

Solution: Upgrade the current default "Contact me for Future Litters" message to be configurable (i.e. "I am expecting a French Bulldog litter in April 2017")

Why I chose it: The work is a low LOE that provides enough value to be an MVP. We plan to test this in beta with a select group of Breeders and get feedback to determine what we can do in future iterations.

Estimated to ship on 3/23/17
