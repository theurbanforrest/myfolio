City Inspired

RISE AND PINE is based in New York City, the city that never sleeps and the mecca for hustlers. From the suit & ties in Midtown to the laid back vibes of the East Village, it is this addictive energy that only those who have been here can understand.

Our designs are a fun representation of the eclectic personalities that make up the Big (Pine)Apple.

THE BIG (PINE)APPLE

A Pineapple wearing a suit hustling around the big city: what's there not to love?

RISE AND PINE aims to capture that Go! Go! Go! rush of energy bundled up into a fun little mascot.
